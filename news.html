6 Sep '24 Grant
Google Cloud research credits awarded to ANLAM-Net Lab
Exciting news! ANLAM-Net Lab has been awarded Google Cloud research credits to support our cutting-edge NLP studies. This generous grant empowers our team to leverage state-of-the-art cloud computing resources for multimedia processing and sentiment analysis projects, enabling faster data processing, more complex model training, and more ambitious challenges in natural language understanding.
28 Aug '24 Milestone
ANLAM-Net Lab is born
ANLAM-Net Lab is officially born. Our team is excited to collaborate with industry leaders on a new project involving multimedia processing and sentiment analysis.
14 Aug '24 Publication
Second conference paper accepted for UBMK 2024
It is with great joy that we announce our second conference paper on Turkish NLP studies has been accepted for UBMK 2024, held in Antalya, Turkiye, between October 26-28, 2024.
