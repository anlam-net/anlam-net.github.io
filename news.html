20 May '25 Publication
Latest work on Turkish sentiment analysis with fine-tuned LLMs
Thrilled to share our latest work on improving Turkish Sentiment Analysis with fine-tuned LLMs. We combined GPT-based relabeling with LoRA fine-tuning on models such as Llama-3 and Gemma, achieving state-of-the-art results on Turkish e-commerce reviews and outperforming commercial LLMs by over 6%. Early Access Paper here: Link.
12 Sep '24 Grant
Funded project by Deanship of Scientific Research (DSR), King Abdulaziz University
We are pleased to announce that our research project has been awarded funding by the Deanship of Scientific Research (DSR) at King Abdulaziz University under grant no. GPIP: 1789-612-2024. Team members: Assoc. Prof. Murat Karakus (Software Engineering, Ankara University), Dr. Rukiye Savran Kiziltepe (Software Engineering, Karadeniz Technical University), Dr. Ercan Ezin (Computer Engineering, Harran University), and Dr. Arwa Mohmmed Basbrain (Computer Science, King Abdulaziz University).
6 Sep '24 Grant
Google Cloud research credits awarded to ANLAM-Net Lab
Exciting news! ANLAM-Net Lab has been awarded Google Cloud research credits to support our cutting-edge NLP studies. This generous grant empowers our team to leverage state-of-the-art cloud computing resources for multimedia processing and sentiment analysis projects, enabling faster data processing, more complex model training, and more ambitious challenges in natural language understanding.
